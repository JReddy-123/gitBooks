gitBooks and More

Welcome To The College Student's Auction Page

GitBooks & MORE is a student focused online marketplace where students and alumni can buy and sell academic materials such as textbooks, calculators, dorm room items, and all other school supplies that are no longer needed by other students.

Why Did We Start This?

College students often struggle with overpriced campus bookstores and unreliable third party sellers, making it difficult to access affordable course materials. While platforms like eBay and Facebook Marketplace support general buying and selling, they are not tailored to the academic community and often lack reliability.

GitBooks & MORE is designed specifically for the UNC Charlotte community, ensuring authenticity, affordability, and easy access to course related materials directly aligned with professorsâ€™ requirements. This makes it not only a cost saving tool, but also a trusted marketplace that addresses the unique needs of the student body.
